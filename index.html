Frequently Asked Questions

How do I get rid of the floating video?

You'd need to buy a premium account for that. Mainly because that's how their business runs - on ads. When logged in, simply click on your profile picture on top right corner, then buy a premium account.

YouTube Music is not available in my country. What do I do?

We can't do much about that for now. But, there is something you can do: write to their support and ask when it will be available for your country.

How does this app protect my privacy?

We are not saving, logging, sending or using any of the data within the player. It's all happening withing the WebView on YouTube Music. Also, the source code is open to public on github.
